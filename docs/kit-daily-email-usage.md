# Daily Email Template – Usage

**File:** `kit-daily-email-template.html`  
**Who gets it:** Existing subscribers (not the welcome email).  
**Purpose:** Teaser + link to today’s article (live on site) + teaser + early-access link to tomorrow’s article.

---

## What to replace each day

In the HTML, search for `<!-- REPLACE:` and update:

1. **Block 1 (Live today)**  
   - Today’s article **title**  
   - Today’s **teaser** (1–2 sentences)  
   - Today’s **URL** in the button `href` (keep `?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_content=today`)

2. **Block 2 (Tomorrow – early access)**  
   - Tomorrow’s article **title**  
   - Tomorrow’s **teaser** (1–2 sentences)  
   - Tomorrow’s **URL** in the button `href` (keep `?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_content=tomorrow-preview`)

## Tone for teasers

- Short, concrete, no hype.  
- One clear benefit or hook (e.g. one pot, no spreadsheets, early access).  
- Optional light edge (“no pile of dishes”, “yours before everyone else”) to match site voice.
