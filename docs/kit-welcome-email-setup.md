# Kit Welcome Email Setup

## Recommended Subject Line
- `You're in. Here's what to expect`

## Preview Text
- `Simple recipes, practical nutrition ideas, and kitchen tips you'll actually use.`

## HTML Template
- Use `docs/kit-welcome-email-template.html`

## Recommended From Settings
- `From name`: `Daily Life Hacks`
- `From email`: use a branded address when available, ideally `hello@daily-life-hacks.com`

## Notes
- This email is designed to feel like the site:
  - warm
  - practical
  - simple
  - no hype
- It avoids promising a lead magnet or attachment that does not exist yet.
- It works as a universal welcome email while the more advanced segmented flows are still being built.
