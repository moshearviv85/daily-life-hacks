## Pre-send gate

Before any outreach:

1. Re-open the target's newest relevant article and confirm the writer or desk
   still covers the subject.
2. Confirm a public contact route from the publication or author's own profile.
   Do not infer or scrape private email addresses.
3. Match exactly one finding to an existing paragraph or unresolved question.
4. Link the methodology, correction note, chart, and CSV.
5. Keep the pitch under 120 words and ask for nothing broader than permission to
   send the data or consideration as a source.
